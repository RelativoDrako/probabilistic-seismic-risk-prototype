## Acceptance criteria

The review is acceptable when:

- it reads as a report about the web app, not as a duplicated copy of the web app;
- each screenshot has an interpretive purpose;
- the reader can understand why the maps, methodology, and evaluation matter;
- no new technical claims are introduced beyond the runnable repository;
- the non-warning, non-deterministic, local-first prototype posture remains clear.
