# Evaluation Scenarios

## Baseline historical scenario

### Purpose
Review how the current bounded workflow behaves over historical evidence.

### Why it matters
It establishes the minimum credible review posture of the prototype.

### Out of scope
Real-time operational behavior.

## Artifact consistency scenario

### Purpose
Verify that reports, plots, maps, and summaries remain coherent with the structured authority.

### Why it matters
It supports publication-safe communication.

### Out of scope
Scientific certification or institutional validation.

## Surface validation scenario

### Purpose
Confirm that the API and web surfaces expose bounded outputs consistently.

### Why it matters
It demonstrates modularity and executive readability.

### Out of scope
Internet-scale service behavior.
