# Evaluation Metrics

## Accuracy-oriented summary

### Why it matters
Provides a bounded view of whether the current baseline can separate prototype classes better than random assignment.

### What it does not imply
It does not imply deterministic predictive validity, production readiness, or public-warning suitability.

## Precision / Recall posture

### Why it matters
Helps explain trade-offs between missed detections and over-triggering within the bounded prototype context.

### What it does not imply
It does not justify operational thresholding without additional governance and scenario work.

## Probability distribution views

### Why it matters
Shows how the model distributes confidence under the present bounded setup.

### What it does not imply
It does not prove calibrated future-state prediction.

## Interpretation boundary

Metrics in this repository are used to support engineering review, not to claim validated public operational performance.
